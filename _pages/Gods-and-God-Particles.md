<h2 style="color:orange"> <u> <em> Gods and God Particles- The Silence Equation </em> </u> </h2>
Raghavan Jayakumar
  
Step beyond the usual science fiction narratives for a journey that dares to be different... for a stunning science fiction premise in a scientifically grounded story in Gods and God Particles- The Silence Equation. In a world altered by authoritarian control and engineered technology addiction, a physicist understands that humanity must restore empathy in the world torn by authoritarian oppression and addiction to technology, in order to take its place in a Universe vested in harmony. In this tale of familial love and close friendships, science meets philosophy, old teachers return to remind us of the right path, human and god-like beings try to find their home in the quiet rhythm of the cosmos and stand against collapse.


Gods and God Particles, a novel Written by a research physicist—grounded in real science: brane cosmology, genetics, and information theory. A novel,  for,  Those Who care!
